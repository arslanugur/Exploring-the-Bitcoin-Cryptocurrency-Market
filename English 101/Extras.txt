🔻 How to value a company
	The dividend Discount Model (DDM) is one of the many methods used to value a company's share price
	
		Formula:
		Share price = Expected Dividend Per Share (EDPS) / (Cost of Capital Equity (CCE) - Divident Growth Rate (DGR))
		
		Let's look at Coca-Cola (NYSE: KO)
		
		Coca-Cola = $1.64 (DPS) / (5.63% (CCE) - 2.52% (DGR)) = $2.73 	---> Current Coca-Cola Stock Price: $46.30 ---> Undervalue
